The Studio

Our Story

Tom Pigeon was founded in 2014 by artist and designer Kirsty Thomas. We enjoy exploring the playful nature of colour, form, abstraction and line and experimenting with process, materials, people and products. We are never bored!

We have collaborated with amazing clients around the world including Tate Modern, The Design Museum, Barbican and Team GB.

I also work in paint and collage and you can browse these works at kirstytanner.com

The Process

Form

Playful, joyful exploration of colour, line and form is at the heart of every Tom Pigeon product.

People

Material
